SOMETIMES THE COST OF TREATMENT CAN BE HARSHER THAN THE DISEASE

At Dr. Reddy’s, we’re working to reduce the burden of both

Modern medicines can be alarmingly expensive, partly because it is difficult to get their complexity and chirality right. Unfortunately, this means that many patients simply cannot afford treatment.

By providing affordable drugs, Dr. Reddy’s ensures that patients around the world get the treatment they need.

Modern medicines can be alarmingly expensive, partly because it is difficult to get their complexity and chirality right. Unfortunately, this means that many patients simply cannot afford treatment.

By providing affordable drugs, Dr. Reddy’s ensures that patients around the world get the treatment they need.

We go where we're needed

Take the case of azacitidine, a drug for MDS, in the USA. Effective as it was, azacitidine was also chemically unstable, making the commercial production of a generic challenging.

Our research team conducted structured experiments to resolve the drug’s instability, enabling production at a commercial scale and improving access and affordability.

Today, Dr. Reddy's azacitidine has been made accessible to nearly half of the azacitidine- treated MDS patients in the United States1

We manage the entire value chain

Our end-to-end capabilities – from producing the active ingredients to developing formulations to distributing them through our streamlined supply chain – allow us to bring down costs.

We also leverage our inherent strength – one of the largest manufacturing bases in India – to provide a distinct cost advantage for patients.

We believe in quality

We believe that the value of a patient’s health is the same around the world.

Which is why we ensure that our products conform to the principle of “One Product, One Quality” across all the countries we serve.
